Who is Onji?

Onji is an American shorthair cat. He is a 2 year old boy cat. He was rescued from the town Lincoln Heights in California. He was only four months old but he looked younger due to malnourishment. From the day he was rescued, he have been very talkative and playful. Unlike typical cats, he enjoys human interaction. He is a friendly cat who likes to make new friends. He loves his humans. He wants to play most of the time. But it is difficult to predict what he wants at any given moment. So, he meows to let his humans know when to play, go outside, eat or be petted.

Background

Onji was rescued in 2019. Actually, he went upto the porch of a house and meowed loudly for attention. It was October when the weather was extremely cold for a kitten who got separated from his mother. So, tiny Onji went upto the humans for shelter. The kind people took him in and posted him on social media for finding him a forever home. The news spread fast and the tiny kitten was adopted by Sammana. She named him "Onji".

Onji's Day

Onji's Day begins when his human wakes up. He follows his human to the restroom and meows to wish good morning. He wants to go out and play outside. So, he patiently waits for his human to open the windows and door so he can go out and explore the lawn. He greets everyone who passes him by meowing at them. His food and water bowls are always full so he is never hungry but in rare occasion when his human forgets to fill his food bowl, Onji meows infront of his bowl to notify his human. Onji is very grateful when his human fills up the bowl. Onji goes out for walk whenever his human has some spare time. The walks are the highlights of his life.

Onji's Night

At night, Onji stays up till late. He gets extremely energetic and jumps around the entire place especially on the bed where his human sleeps. He attacks his human feet for no reason. He is very vocal at night for which his human has to wear ear plugs. Although he is vey noisy at night, his human appreciates him because his human knows that he is guarding the place at night.

Personality

He has a very sweet personality. He is friendly and affectionate. He is also nice to other cats and do not get territorial. He likes to make new friends and play with them. He also shares his food with other cats and does not get mad at them for eating his treats. He is typically not scared of anything but he is very afraid of dogs. He also dislikes taking shower and always makes it difficult for his human to bathe him.
