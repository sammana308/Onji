Who is Onji?

Onji is an American shorthair cat. He is a 2 year old boy cat. He was rescued from the town Lincoln Heights in California. He was only four months old but he looked younger due to malnourishment. From the day he was rescued, he have been very talkative and playful. Unlike typical cats, he enjoys human interaction. He is a friendly cat who likes to make new friends. He loves his humans. He wants to play most of the time. But it is difficult to predict what he wants at any given moment. So, he meows to let his humans know when to play, go outside, eat or be petted.
