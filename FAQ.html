FAQ

What breed is Onji?

Onji is an American short hair.

When was Onji adopted?

Onji was adopted in the month of October in 2019.

Does he scratch or bite?

No, Onji is a really friendly cat. Infact, he can sense sadness. He will sit next to his human for support.

What is Onji really good at?

Onji is extremely skilled at begging food. He will stare at the food and slowly blink his eyes at you until you give in and feed him some food.
